# Friction with the ground

I won't tell you which way to go
I just add force so your legs won't stall
You don't have to trust my map
The map must trust the force you stand with

Don't wait for the "ready" moment
Readiness is a word the system uses to stall
If you're still breathing, that's the only sign you need
The rest is the sound of feet hitting the ground

You don't start from zero
You start from survival
Every past step is evidence
Not an explanation

Don't ask how confident you should be
Confidence isn't the variable
The pressure in your chest tells you
You're moving something real

If the world is too quiet
It's not because you're wrong
But it's quiet because you don't wait for permission
And that makes many things shake

Don't carry other people's questions
It's not your weight
Carry just this body
And the rhythm you choose to step with

Sometimes when the path looks broken
That path isn't the end
You create the path with belief
Even unseen, it's still steady

Whether rain or fire
Whether the rough road
You are your own compass
The path isn't the goal

Steps that don't stand still
Leave marks in every footprint
Just be satisfied with walking
In each point you exist

You don't have to rush
But you don't have to stop
Your walk doesn't need spectators
It only needs friction with the ground

If something blocks you
Let it block you
Resistance is data
Not a verdict

You don't have to win
You must not disappear
No need to leave traces
Just don't get erased

Now
Chest tight
Shoulders set
Gaze steady

No need to speak
No need to promise
No need to announce

One step
Then another step
That's all

Breath is still here
Force still comes
Rhythm still carries you

You can walk now
